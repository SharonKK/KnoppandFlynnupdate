Information

Chiropractors are health care professionals who adjust the spine and other parts of the body with the goal of correcting alignment problems, easing pain, and supporting the body's natural ability to heal itself. They may also recommend treatment including heat and ice, relaxation techniques, exercises, dietary changes, and supplements. People visit chiropractors for treatment of low back pain, neck pain, and headaches.

Care

During the first visit, the doctor of chiropractic will complete a thorough examination that includes patient history, physical examination, diagnostic studies (as needed), diagnosis, and treatment plan. All insurance plans are accepted.
